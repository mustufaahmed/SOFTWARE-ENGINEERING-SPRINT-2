KARACHI CLUB WELCOMES NEW MEMBERS ACROSS ALL CATEGORIES

We have various membership packages available to suit all ages and lifestyles with NO joining fee. You don't need to know an existing member to join. We can show you around and even play a few holes to get you familiar with the course and clubhouse. Members enjoy many facilities at Karachi Club including individual and team competitions, thriving social calendar, function room hire, superb catering, professional coaching, golf shop and drinks discount in the bar! We also run a number of popular golf trips each year that are great fun. We offer 6 and 7 day memberships as well as discounts for younger members. See below for details and visit our membership rates page for the latest prices and offers. For more information on any of our membership packages please contact the club office on 0161 998 4738. Alternatively you can use our online contact form or apply online using the form on the Membership Rates page.

Family Membership

Family are always welcome at Karachi Club and with a new club house and brand new ladies locker room now is the perfect time to join. If you are under 30 years old further discounts apply!! Benefits of a Famnily Membership at NGC:

  • Weekday and Weekend Competitions
  • EWGA Competitions
  • Business Ladies
  • Team Matches
  • Bridge Evenings (Men welcome too)
  • Friday Frolics
  • Active Social scene
  • And don't forget we have a beautiful golf course playable ALL year round!!
